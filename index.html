What awaits

A world of discovery in your pocket

Three portals. 195 countries. Thousands of surprises. Atlas turns geography into an adventure you never want to put down.

🌍

Three Portals

Explore the Continents, dive into the Oceans, or reach for the Stars. Each portal is its own universe of wonder and knowledge.

📖

Learn in A-Mode

Four interactive slides per country — place it on the map, meet its national animal, cook its national dish, and hear its Capital Rhyme.

🏆

Quiz Ladder

Climb Easy, Medium, Hard, and Master tiers. Every pass earns Atlas Orbs. Master a country and earn its Country Spotlight forever.

🐾

195 National Pets

Every country hides a unique animal companion — from Mexico's Axolotl to Japan's Shiba Inu Pup. Pass the Hard quiz to unlock yours.

🏠

Your Base Camp

Choose from 50 elite house styles — a Kyoto pagoda, a Polynesian bungalow, or a Bavarian cottage. Hang travel photos. Make it home.

🛍️

Regional Market

Spend Atlas Orbs on 1,365 culturally authentic clothing items and accessories sourced from every corner of the globe.

How it works

Learn, quiz, earn, explore

Every country is a journey. Every journey earns Atlas Orbs. Every Orb opens something new.

1

Pick a Country

Spin the globe and choose any of 195 countries — from Andorra to Zimbabwe.

2

Learn in A-Mode

Four slides walk you through its location, national animal, national dish, and capital city rhyme.

3

Test in Q-Mode

Pass four quiz tiers to unlock pets, Atlas Orbs, and your Country Spotlight.

4

Explore & Build

Teleport to 3D landmarks, shop the Regional Market, and grow your global base camp.
