How it works

Choose a portal. Learn. Quiz. Earn. Build.

Every portal is its own adventure. Every quiz you pass earns Atlas Orbs — the points that unlock clothing, houses, and more.

1

Choose Your Portal

Enter the Continents portal to explore 195 countries, the Oceans portal to discover the deep, or the Stars portal to journey through space.

2

Learn in A-Mode

Interactive slides walk you through each topic — maps, national animals, capital cities, ocean depths, celestial facts, and more.

3

Quiz & Earn Orbs

Climb Easy, Medium, Hard, and Master quiz tiers. Every tier you pass awards Atlas Orbs — your in-game points — plus pets and Country Spotlights.

4

Spend & Customize

Spend your Orbs on 1,365+ culturally authentic clothing items, or upgrade your base camp with 50 elite house styles from around the world.
