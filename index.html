What awaits

A world of discovery in your pocket

Three worlds to explore. Hundreds of quizzes to conquer. The more you learn, the more you unlock.

Three Portals

Explore 195 countries in Continents, discover what lurks beneath the waves in Oceans, or launch into the universe in Stars. Each portal is its own world.

Learn Before You Quiz

Before every quiz, Atlas teaches you everything — where a country is on the map, its national animal, its famous food, and a rhyme to remember the capital.

Quiz Ladder

Four levels: Easy, Medium, Hard, and Master. Each one you pass earns Atlas Orbs — your points. Reach Master and that place is marked as yours forever.

195 National Pets

Every country has a hidden animal companion. Ace the Hard quiz to unlock it — from Mexico's Axolotl to Japan's Shiba Inu. How many can you collect?

Your Base Camp

Your own home inside Atlas. Pick a house style — a Japanese pagoda, a beach bungalow, or a mountain lodge — and fill it with your travel collection.

Regional Market

Spend your Atlas Orbs on 1,365+ outfits and accessories from cultures all over the world. Dress your explorer however you like.

How it works

Pick a portal. Learn. Quiz. Earn. Build.

Atlas Orbs are the points you earn every time you pass a quiz. The more you explore, the more you unlock.

1

Pick a Portal

Tap Continents, Oceans, or Stars. Each one has its own topics, quizzes, and rewards — so there's always somewhere new to go.

2

Learn First

Atlas shows you everything before you're tested — cool facts, maps, animals, and things you've probably never heard before. No guessing blind.

3

Take the Quiz

Answer questions across four levels — Easy, Medium, Hard, and Master. Every level you beat earns Atlas Orbs, your points in the game.

4

Spend Your Orbs

Use your Orbs to grab new outfits for your explorer or upgrade your base camp with a new house. The better you do, the cooler your setup gets.
